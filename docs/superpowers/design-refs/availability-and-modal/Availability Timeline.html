CampWatch · Field Notes · Availability, at a glance

One axis, every campground on it.

All campgrounds share one time axis; click any campground to fan it out into its individual sites, each a thin row on the same axis. The tags are per site: every site you watch is a ★ favorite, a ◇ worthwhile, or a plain other — so you can tell your lakefront favorite from the overflow site by the road. Favorites sort to the top of each campground, carry a clay star, and their openings get a clay ring so a favorite site coming free pops instantly. The summary row tallies the mix (★2 ◇1 ·1) and flags when a favorite is open.
